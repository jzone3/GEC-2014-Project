The Produce Caboose brings family farming directly to the consumer in the form of a "traveling train" traversing the United States' extensive railway networks.

The business takes an eco-friendly initiative by reuisng all organic materials and making sure that no food goes to waste. Unsold produce will be fermented in a section of the last boxcar with yeast in an anaerobic environment. The resulting ethanol solution will then be converted into biodiesel to power the Produce Caboose.
